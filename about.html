About us

A UK-based technology company

TA Solutions Ltd focuses on building and operating modern digital platforms that combine strong product thinking, robust engineering and regulatory compliance.

Who we are

TA Solutions Ltd is a privately held company registered in the United Kingdom. We work at the intersection of digital products, infrastructure and operations, with a particular interest in creator platforms, real estate technology and healthcare systems.

Our background spans product design, software development and service operations. That mix allows us to design platforms that don’t just look good on paper but can be managed day-to-day by real teams in real environments.

We work in an iterative and collaborative way – shipping usable versions early, gathering feedback, and continuously improving based on real usage and performance.

Our principles

  • Innovation that solves real problems.
    Every product starts with a clearly defined problem, not just a trend or buzzword.
  • Compliance and security by design.
    We aim to embed governance, privacy and security into the architecture from the outset.
  • Scalable, reliable platforms.
    We favour pragmatic architecture and clarity over unnecessary complexity.
  • Human-focused development.
    Products are built around the needs of creators, operators and customers – not the tech itself.
