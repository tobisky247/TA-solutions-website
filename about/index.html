About us

A UK-based technology company building modern digital platforms

We design, build and operate scalable products for creators, businesses, operators and teams who need more than a simple website — they need a digital platform.

Who we are

TA Solutions Ltd is a digital technology company based in the United Kingdom. We specialise in taking platform ideas from early concept to fully-operational products — blending product strategy, design, engineering and long-term operational support.

Our work spans creator platforms, AI-powered tools, marketplaces, booking systems, and digital products designed for scale and reliability.

We partner with founders, operators, early-stage teams and established organisations who want to build modern, user-centred technology with real-world performance.

How we work

We combine product thinking with practical delivery. That means defining clear problem statements, mapping user journeys, validating assumptions, and delivering fast, high-quality releases with a focus on usability and long-term maintainability.

Every project is approached holistically — from UX and accessibility, to engineering reliability, privacy, compliance, infrastructure and ongoing operations.
