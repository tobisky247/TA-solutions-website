About

TA Solutions Ltd

TA Solutions Ltd is a UK-based technology company focused on building, running and scaling modern digital platforms. We combine product strategy, design, engineering and operations into one partner.

Technology with real-world impact

Our work is grounded in real operational challenges – creator payouts, compliance, user safety, performance, and the day-to-day realities of running live products. We build platforms designed to be lived with, not just launched.

  • Platform thinking: We design for multi-sided ecosystems, not just single features.
  • Long-term view: We care about resilience, maintainability and the teams who run products.
  • Creator & business focus: Our products are shaped alongside real users and partners.

Where we focus

We actively build and manage products in:

  • Creator monetisation & subscription platforms
  • Real estate workflows & property operations
  • Healthcare, care delivery & compliance tooling
  • Document and AI-assisted workflows
